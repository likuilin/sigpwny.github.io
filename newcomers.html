Info for first timers

Welcome to SIGPwny! We are glad that you found us, and we hope you are ready to start hacking! Outlined here are a bunch of resources to get started.

  1. Join our discord here, at sigpwny.com/discord to talk with members of the club. Once you are there, read #rules and #announcements for any recent info
  2. Flip through the presentation below!

Our second meeting of the year will generally be "Introduction to Shells". If you're joining us after that, please learn what a shell is and make sure you have a bash one on your computer.

Here are some other learning resources for getting started:

learn shell (good for starting): http://overthewire.org/wargames/bandit/
in-depth on general programming productivity tools: https://c4cs.github.io/#schedule
pwn: http://pwnable.kr/
cryptography: Crypto 101 or CryptoPals
web: http://overthewire.org/wargames/natas/
reversing/embedded: http://microcorruption.com/
